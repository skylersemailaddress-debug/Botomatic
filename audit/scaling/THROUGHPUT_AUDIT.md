# Throughput Audit

## Purpose

Review orchestration throughput, workload concurrency, and execution stability under sustained platform usage.

## Focus Areas

- orchestration throughput
- concurrent execution
- queue pressure
- retry amplification
- tenant workload isolation
- runtime stability

## Risks

- throughput collapse
- retry amplification
- cross-tenant workload instability

## Direction

```text
observable workload
-> governed concurrency
-> stable orchestration
-> measurable throughput
```
