# Blueprint Registry

Botomatic includes blueprint coverage for common commercial products including:
- websites and landing pages
- SaaS dashboard, CRM, booking
- marketplace, ecommerce, membership, LMS
- job board, directory, customer portal
- internal admin/workflow/analytics
- event/media/document/inventory/support
- real estate, field service, nonprofit ops, campaign ops
- AI chatbot and AI agent workflow apps

Blueprints provide defaults for pages, entities, workflows, roles, integrations, launch criteria, and no-placeholder rules.

Registry contract:
- Each blueprint must include required questions, safe assumptions, risky assumptions, acceptance criteria, launch criteria, and validation rules.
- Blueprints are used to seed spec analysis and planning but must be overridden by explicit user requirements.
- Blueprint defaults must not bypass high-risk clarification requirements.
