# Autonomy and Clarification Policy

Clarification behavior:
- Importance 9-10: must ask, build blocked
- Importance 7-8: recommend default, ask approval unless delegated
- Importance 4-6: auto-decide only if safe and delegated
- Importance 1-3: safe silent decision allowed, record assumption

Question quality policy:
- Ask concise grouped questions.
- Prefer 3-7 high-leverage questions.
- Never silently decide high-risk categories.

High-risk categories (must ask):
- payments and billing obligations
- legal/compliance and regulated workflows
- auth/security model and permissions
- data deletion/retention rules
- external API cost exposure
- public/private visibility controls
- irreversible business rules
